🎯 PROJECT OVERVIEW

Problem:
  SNAP recipients have limited budgets and don't know nutrition guidelines.
  They buy cheap, low-nutrition foods → malnutrition.

Solution:
  AI recommendation system that suggests the best foods for them based on:
  • Their nutritional needs (USDA guidelines)
  • Their budget (never exceeds available funds)
  • Their health conditions (diabetes, vegetarian, etc.)
  • What similar people bought (collaborative filtering)

Impact:
  Families can get 30-50% better nutrition within the same budget.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🧠 HOW THE AI WORKS

Four recommendation algorithms combined (hybrid):

1. CONTENT-BASED (40% weight)
   "What nutrition are you missing?"
   → Analyzes gaps in your diet
   → Recommends foods that fill those gaps
   → Prioritizes cost-per-nutrient value

2. COLLABORATIVE (30% weight)
   "What did similar users buy?"
   → Finds people like you
   → Sees what they purchased
   → Recommends their favorites

3. KNOWLEDGE-BASED (filtering)
   "What are your restrictions?"
   → Diabetes? Avoid high-glycemic foods
   → Vegetarian? Skip meat
   → Lactose intolerant? No dairy

4. BUDGET OPTIMIZATION
   "Can you afford it?"
   → Never exceeds your budget
   → Maximizes nutrition within budget

Result: Personalized recommendations ranked by score
