Support pricing

Mixed grocery boxes keep subsidy strategy separate from the regular shelf.

These are still bundled boxes rather than isolated discounted items. The mix is designed to preserve a real grocery basket across grains, produce, and protein.

How this section works

Subsidized support lives as a product line, not as scattered price overrides.

The prototype keeps support pricing legible by packaging complete baskets. That lets the page show a mainstream catalog while still making affordability a visible part of the service model.

  • Campus pickup is one access point, but boxes also fit apartment and community use.
  • Each box bundles a grain or pantry base, produce, and protein.
  • Launch pricing would still need live retail, labor, cold storage, and SNAP or EBT validation.

Locker operations

Temperature-zoned pickup that works for dorms, apartments, and community nodes.

The lockers are the bridge between citywide coverage and practical pickup. They support short-dwell groceries, scheduled orders, and after-class or after-work retrieval without requiring every user to be routed through a campus flow.
