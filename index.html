Food that covers the basics.

Each pack combines protein, staples, and produce in a format that fits student routines, older-adult homes, and family households.

  • EBT and subsidy friendly
  • Built for students, older adults, and family households
  • Priced below retail through bulk purchasing
  • Vegan, vegetarian, and non-veg options
  • Student pricing available with verified .edu logins
⚠️ Bulk ordering for subsidized boxes is available exclusively for SNAP/EBT cardholders and income-qualified households. Standard single-box purchases are open to all.
Weekly Packs

Choose the pack that fits the household.

These packs are sized for actual use: quick-prep student bundles, gentler 2-person senior packs, and fuller 4-person family support packs. EBT is accepted at every hub, and verified .edu accounts can unlock student pricing.
